About Company

J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY, formerly known as "THE NEVIL AGENCY," has been a steadfast presence in the industry since its inception in August 2021. Founded by the visionary Mr. Yugal, this proprietorship firm has continuously evolved to meet the ever-changing demands of the workforce and security sectors. With a strong commitment to excellence, J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable manpower solutions, both skilled and unskilled, as well as top-notch security services. Our team comprises dedicated professionals, including personnel, bouncers, and ex-servicemen, who are meticulously trained to ensure the safety and efficiency of our clients' operations. With a foundation built on integrity and a passion for service, J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY stands ready to provide tailor-made solutions to meet your organization's needs.


Specialization

Specializing in both skilled and unskilled manpower solutions, as well as top-tier security services provided by trained personnel, bouncers, and ex-servicemen, we are committed to enhancing the efficiency and safety of businesses across various sectors. With a track record of reliability and a dedication to excellence, J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY is your go-to partner for all your workforce and security needs.

About MD

Mr. Yugal
Founder (MD)

Mr. Yugal, the Managing Director of J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY, hails from 190/2, Shiv Colony, Beri Gate, Jhajjar-124103. With a vision for a brighter future and a commitment to the company's continuous development, he founded the company initially under the name "THE NEVIL AGENCY" in August 2021. Recognizing the evolving demands of the industry and the need to expand services, Mr. Yugal decided to change the company's name in February 2023 to J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY.
Under his leadership, the company has excelled in providing top-notch manpower solutions and security services to a diverse clientele, including limited companies, private limited firms, and government agencies. Mr. Yugal's dedication to excellence, combined with his foresight, has enabled the company to thrive and adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the workforce and security sectors, making J.S. MANPOWER RESOURCES & SECURITY AGENCY a trusted partner for businesses and government entities alike.
